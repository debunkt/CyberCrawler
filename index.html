◈ HOW TO PLAY

Movement

▲▼◀▶ D-pad on screen, or swipe the map.

Arrow keys / WASD / hjkl work on keyboard.

Walk into an enemy to melee attack.

Actions

HACK — Quickhack nearest visible enemy (costs 15 EN, range 10).

SHOOT — Shoot nearest enemy with your ranged weapon.

TAKE — Pick up item on your tile.

NEXT — Descend stairs when standing on >

· — Wait / skip turn (regen 2 EN).

INV — Open inventory (or press I).

In Inventory

Tap USE on weapons/armor to equip them.

Tap USE on consumables to use them.

Cyberware auto-installs into free slots.

Status Bars

HP — Health. Reach 0 and you're flatlined.

EN — Energy. Used for hacking. Regens +2 every 5 turns.

Enemies

G Maelstrom Ganger — Melee brute. Weak to hacking.

N NCPD Officer — Shoots from range. Alert and accurate.

D Security Drone — Flying, ranged. High hack defense.

R Netrunner — Hacks you at range. Drains HP + EN.

S Corp Soldier — Tough, armed. Mid-to-late game.

M MaxTac Elite — Cyborg police. Very dangerous.

B Boss — Arasaka Operative. Final floor guardian.

Tiles

> Stairs down — Use NEXT button to descend.

▣ Terminal — Flavor only (no interaction yet).

Goal

Descend all 10 sectors of Night City and crack the Arasaka vault. Good luck, choom.
